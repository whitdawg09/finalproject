Fun facts!

  • The walls of the deli are adorned with photos, notes, and memorabilia from customers, including celebrities and visitors who have fallen in love with its charm.

  • The deli’s famous Giugni Juice is a secret family recipe that has remained unchanged for decades. It's so popular that many customers buy bottles of it to take home.

  • The Giugni family has been actively involved in running the deli for over 100 years, with recipes and traditions passed down through generations.

    • 1918-2024!

    Giugni's Deli, located in St. Helena, California, has a rich history rooted in family and tradition. Established by the Giugni family, the deli began as a small grocery store serving the local community. Over the years, it evolved into the beloved sandwich shop it is today, known for its fresh ingredients and signature "Giugni Juice." The deli has remained a family-run business for generations, maintaining its original charm and commitment to quality. Its longstanding presence in the heart of Napa Valley has made it a cherished institution, drawing both locals and visitors who come to experience a piece of culinary history. Giugni’s blend of tradition and timeless recipes keeps its legacy alive, making it a staple in St. Helena for over a century.
